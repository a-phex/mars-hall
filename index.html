About Ash Marshall

I lead multimedia production across EMEA, turning complex ideas into clear, engaging video content that supports marketing, partners and internal teams.

My role sits at the intersection of creative production, stakeholder management and technical delivery — owning projects end-to-end from concept through to final distribution.

I produce and manage:

Webinar and livestream content (Teams, OBS, studio & remote workflows)

Partner and customer case-study videos across multiple countries

Thought leadership, product showcases and internal communications

Event video, photography and post-event content

I work closely with marketing managers, partners and subject-matter experts to translate written case studies and ideas into video formats that are practical, scalable and repeatable across regions.

Alongside production, I focus on improving workflows, standardising processes and making video easier for non-video teams to engage with, without compromising on quality.

I’m particularly interested in roles where video is treated as a strategic communication tool, not just a creative output.

Skills & tools: Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, DaVinci Resolve, Colour & Sound Mixing, Lighting & Gaffing, Final Cut, Touch Designer, Cinema 4D, OBS, Rode Video Caster
